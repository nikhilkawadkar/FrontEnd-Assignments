About Cropdeals

We will act as a bridge between the Farmer and the Dealer (Crop purchaser). All you need to do is selecting whether you are a Farmer or the Dealer, signing up with details and the payment information. There is no need for the farmer to carry the crop till the market, paying unexpected commissions and wait for proper price from the Dealers.
